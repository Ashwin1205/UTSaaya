Saaya XI

"This year, we did the story of Joy Lobo from 3 Idiots. As a a part of the south asian community, it was a honor for us to be able to depict the struggles of mental health, especially in academia, and continue to raise awareness for it. Because it’s an issue that affects so many people, we felt fortunate that our show made waves in the community and was able to discuss a valuable message." - Deeksha and Natasha

Saaya X

"This year, our story was based on Hichki - a film depicting how outcasted kids were able to overcome their struggles when a young teacher, who battled Tourette’s, showed them the light. This story of hope and hard work is important to us because it was Saaya’s story. It showed us that no matter what challenges we were faced with, we could remain dedicated, strong and passionate about dance and telling this story.

This year's piece is dedicated to Keerat Baweja, our beautiful, brilliant late captain. We love you. This is also dedicated to all the teachers who have inspired students and changed lives." - Suvi and Shweta
